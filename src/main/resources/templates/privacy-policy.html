Privacy Policy

Last Modified: 16.01.2024.

Welcome to the SmartPick e-commerce shopping platform. We value your trust and aim to transparently communicate how we handle your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices and commitments to ensure the security and privacy of your information.

1. Data Collection

Your registration and purchase-related information allow us to provide top-notch services. We collect only essential personal data necessary for order processing.

2. Data Usage

Personal data is solely used for order processing and enhancing your user experience. Additionally, we employ advanced AI technology to personalize services, tailoring them to your needs.

3. Data Sharing

Your data remains secure with us. We do not share it with third parties to ensure complete privacy.

4. Data Security

We implement state-of-the-art security measures, including role-based authorization, authentication, and password hashing, to ensure the complete protection of your data.

5. User Rights

We respect your rights. You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data, all of which can be easily done through your user account on the account page.

6. Cookies

To enhance your experience, we do not use cookies on our platform.

7. Age restriction

Our platform is accessible to all users, regardless of age.

8. Notification of Changes

Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be transparently communicated to you via email to keep you informed about our practices.

Please carefully read this document. If you have additional questions or concerns, feel free to contact us.
